# Privacy Policy

EmpirioLabs takes user privacy and data protection seriously. This policy describes how this plugin handles your data when you use EmpirioLabs models in Dify.

## 1. AI Model API Interaction Data

EmpirioLabs does not retain the content of your prompts, messages, request bodies, or response bodies for inspection or any other retention purpose. Your prompts and the model outputs are processed to fulfill your request and to return a result to you. They are not stored for training or review.

Operational metadata required to run the service and bill usage (such as request identifiers, model name, token counts, latency, and status) is kept so that usage and billing can be reported accurately. This metadata does not include the content of your prompts or responses.

## 2. Credentials

Your EmpirioLabs API key is provided by you and stored by your Dify instance to authenticate requests. The key is sent only to the EmpirioLabs API over HTTPS and is not shared with any other party.

## 3. Information Security

Requests to the EmpirioLabs API are transmitted over encrypted connections. Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect data in transit and at rest. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

## 4. Use of Information

Information is used to provide and operate the service, to process your requests, to meter and bill usage, to prevent abuse, and to maintain reliability and security.

## 5. Information Sharing

EmpirioLabs does not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version of the EmpirioLabs privacy policy is published at https://empiriolabs.ai.

## 7. Contact

For questions about this policy, contact us through the channels listed at https://empiriolabs.ai.
